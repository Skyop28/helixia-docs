The Bingo Event is a game hosted by the **Sugari Beta** bot. It's a straightforward bingo game where participants are automatically tracked.

## How to Participate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the game">
    When a Bingo event is hosted in the Bingo Event channel, follow the Sugari Beta bot's instructions to join the game. The specific join method depends on the Sugari bot's commands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play the game">
    Play the bingo game as normal. Sugari Beta handles all the game mechanics — marking numbers, checking cards, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participants are tracked automatically">
    Helixia automatically detects participants when the Sugari bot mentions players in the game. You don't need to do anything extra — just being in the game is enough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Game ends">
    When Sugari Beta posts the "Game Over" screen, Helixia awards quest credit to all participants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quest Credit

Quest credit is given to **every player mentioned by the Sugari bot** during the game. You receive credit once the game ends with the "Game Over" announcement — not during the game.

You don't need to win the bingo game. Participating is enough.

## When It Happens

Bingo events are initiated by staff members, typically around **8:00 PM IST** or later. The exact time may vary from day to day.

<Tip>
  If you're online in the evening, keep an eye on the Bingo Event channel around 8 PM IST. It's one of the easiest ways to complete your daily event quest.
</Tip>
