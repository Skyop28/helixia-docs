The Tea Event is a word game hosted by the **Mudae** bot. There are several variants — Black Tea, Green Tea, Red Tea, Yellow Tea, and Cocktail Tea — but they all work the same way from your perspective as a participant.

## How to Participate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Watch for the start message">
    When a Tea event begins, the Mudae bot posts an announcement embed in the Tea Event channel. The title will say something like "The Black Teaword will start!" (the color varies).
  </Step>
  <Step title="React with ✅ within 15 seconds">
    As soon as you see the start message, react with the ✅ checkmark emoji. You must react **within 15 seconds** of the message being posted to be counted as a participant.

    This is the critical step for quest credit — if you react after 15 seconds, you won't be tracked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play the game">
    The game itself involves typing words that contain a specific group of 3 letters within a time limit. You lose HP when the timer runs out. Last person standing wins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Event ends">
    When the game ends, Mudae posts a message announcing the winner. At this point, quest credit is awarded to all tracked participants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quest Credit

Quest credit is given to **everyone who reacted within the first 15 seconds** of the start message. You receive credit after the event ends, not immediately when you react.

<Warning>
  You must react within **15 seconds**. Late reactions are not counted. Be ready to react quickly when you see the announcement.
</Warning>

Tea events are initiated by staff members, typically around **5:30 PM IST** or later. The exact time may vary from day to day.
