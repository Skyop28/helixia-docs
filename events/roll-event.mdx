The Roll Event is a high-energy, bot-hosted competition where players race to roll a specific number. The first person to match the target number wins **500,000 Pokécoins**.

## Schedule

Roll Events are automatically hosted **twice daily**:
- **5:00 PM IST**
- **4:00 AM IST**

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Announcement">
    The bot pings the event role and posts an announcement in the Roll Event channel. The announcement states the prize and instructions. The channel is locked at this point — you can't type yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join by reacting">
    React with ✅ on the announcement message to signal you want to participate. The event starts once **20 players** have reacted.

    If 20 players don't react within 10 minutes, the event is cancelled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Target number is revealed">
    Once enough players have joined, the bot "rolls" a random number between **1 and 100**. This is the target number that players need to match. The channel is unlocked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start rolling">
    Type `.roll` to roll a random number between 1 and 100. Keep rolling until someone hits the target. There's no limit to how many times you can roll.

    The channel will be very active — many people rolling at once is expected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Winner announced">
    The instant someone rolls the target number, the channel is locked and the winner is announced. The prize of **500,000 PC** is automatically added to the winner's balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quest Credit

**Every player who used `.roll` during the event** receives credit for the "Participate in 1 Daily Event" quest — not just the winner. Simply rolling at least once is enough to complete your event quest for the day.

## Safety Timeout

If nobody rolls the target number within **15 minutes**, the event ends without a winner. All participants still receive quest credit for participating.

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be fast">
    The Roll Event is a race. The faster you can type `.roll` and hit Enter, the more attempts you get. Every roll is a new random number — persistence pays off.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="React early">
    The event can't start until 20 people react. React as soon as you see the announcement to help get the event going quickly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="You don't need to win">
    Even if you don't hit the target number, participating completes your daily event quest. That's 30,000 PC just for showing up and rolling a few times.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
