Giveaways are periodic events where Pokécoins, mystery boxes, or other prizes are given away to lucky winners. They're hosted by staff and appear in the server with a join button.

## How to Join a Giveaway

When a giveaway is active, you'll see a message with the prize details and a **Join** button. Click it to enter.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find an active giveaway">
    Giveaway announcements appear in the server with details about the prize, number of winners, and when it ends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Join button">
    Press the join button on the giveaway message. The bot will check your eligibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the result">
    If you meet all requirements, you're entered. When the giveaway ends, winners are randomly selected and announced.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

To join a giveaway, you must meet these requirements:

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| **Account age** | Your Discord account must be at least **1 month old** |
| **Messages** | You must have at least **50 total messages** in the server |
| **Self roles** | You must have at least **3 self-assigned roles** from the server's role selection |
| **Special role** (if applicable) | Some giveaways require a specific role to enter |

If you don't meet one or more requirements, the bot will show you exactly which ones you're missing.

## Giveaway Details

Each giveaway displays:

- **Prize** — what the winner(s) receive
- **Number of winners** — how many people will win
- **End time** — when the giveaway closes (shown as both a countdown and an exact time)
- **Host** — who created the giveaway
- **Required role** — if a specific role is needed, or "Everyone" if anyone can join
- **Participant count** — updated on the join button

## After Joining

Once you've joined:
- You'll see a confirmation message saying you've entered
- A **Leave** button appears if you change your mind
- You can check if you're entered by clicking the join button again

## Winning

When a giveaway ends:
- Winners are randomly selected from all eligible participants
- The prize is automatically added to the winners' accounts
- Winners are announced in the giveaway message

<Tip>
  Make sure you meet the requirements **before** a giveaway goes live. Having your self-roles set up, enough messages, and an old enough account means you can join instantly when a giveaway appears.
</Tip>
