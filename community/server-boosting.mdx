Boosting the Helixia Discord server shows your support for the community and comes with ongoing rewards. Server boosters receive regular Pokécoin payments as a thank-you for their contribution.

## How It Works

When you boost the server, you're added to the booster tracking system. Boosters receive **weekly Pokécoin payments** for as long as their boost is active.

The exact weekly amount depends on the server's current configuration and may vary.

## Booster Perks

| Perk | Details |
|---|---|
| **Weekly Pokécoins** | Automatic weekly payments added to your balance |
| **Booster recognition** | You're tracked as an active booster |
| **Discord perks** | Standard Discord Nitro boosting perks (custom emoji slots, server banner, etc.) |

## How to Boost

Boosting is done through Discord's built-in boost feature:

1. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) the Helixia server name
2. Select **Boost This Server**
3. Follow Discord's prompts to complete the boost

Once your boost is active, the bot automatically starts tracking your booster status and scheduling payments.

<Tip>
  Server boosting benefits the entire community — more boosts mean more custom emojis, better audio quality, and a higher upload limit for everyone. Plus, you get paid weekly Pokécoins just for keeping your boost active.
</Tip>
