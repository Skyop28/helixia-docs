Achievements are long-term milestones that reward you for sustained activity in the server. Unlike daily quests that reset every day, achievements are permanent — once unlocked, they stay on your profile forever.

## How to View Achievements

Run `.info` and click the **Achievements** button. This shows:
- Your overall progress (e.g., "12/26 unlocked")
- Total Pokécoins earned from achievements
- A progress bar for each category with your next milestone
- Buttons to explore each category in detail

## Categories

There are **six achievement categories**, each with multiple tiers. Higher tiers have harder thresholds but much larger rewards.

### Messages

Earn achievements by sending messages in the tracked whitelisted channel.

| Milestone | Threshold | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 🥉 Bronze | 500 messages | 250,000 PC |
| 🥈 Silver | 1,000 messages | 500,000 PC |
| 🥇 Gold | 5,000 messages | 2,000,000 PC |
| 💎 Diamond | 10,000 messages | 5,000,000 PC |

</Note>

## How Achievements Are Awarded

Achievements are checked automatically as you do things in the server. When you hit a milestone:

1. The achievement is unlocked on your profile
2. The Pokécoin reward is added to your balance
3. You receive a DM notification (if your DMs are open)
4. The unlock is logged in the server's achievement log channel

<Tip>
  Achievements are some of the highest-value rewards in the server. A single Diamond-tier achievement can be worth 2,000,000 to 5,000,000 PC. Focus on the categories where you're closest to the next milestone.
</Tip>
