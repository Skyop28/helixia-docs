Ready to put your Pokécoins on the line? Here's how to go from zero to your first gamble.

## Starting a Gamble

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to a gambling channel">
    Gambling commands only work in the designated gambling channels. If you try to use `.gamble` elsewhere, the bot will tell you where to go.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Challenge someone">
    ```
    .gamble @opponent amount
    ```

    For example:
    ```
    .gamble @Awnish 100k
    ```

    You can use shorthand: `50k` (50,000), `500k` (500,000), `1m` (1,000,000).

    The minimum wager is **50,000 PC**. Both you and your opponent must have enough balance to cover the wager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a game type">
    A dropdown menu appears with four options:
    - **High/Low** — pick HIGH or LOW, then both roll
    - **Death Roll** — take turns rolling in a shrinking range
    - **Buckshot Roulette** — choose to shoot yourself or your opponent
    - **Pokémon Rand** — random Pokémon stat battles

    Select the game you want to play.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a format">
    Select how many rounds you want to play:
    - **Best of 1 (BO1)** — single round, fast
    - **Best of 3 (BO3)** — first to 2 rounds wins
    - **Best of 5 (BO5)** — first to 3 rounds wins (Pokémon Rand only)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opponent accepts or rejects">
    Your opponent sees the challenge with all the details — game type, format, and wager. They have **60 seconds** to click **Accept** or **Reject**.

    If they don't respond in time, the challenge expires automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Game begins">
    Once accepted, both players' wagers are locked in escrow and the game starts. Follow the on-screen prompts — buttons, rolls, and choices are all handled through Discord's interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

Before you can gamble, make sure:

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| **Minimum wager** | 50,000 PC |
| **Sufficient balance** | Both players need enough coins |
| **No active session** | You can't be in another gamble |
| **Not a bot** | You can't gamble against bots |
| **Not yourself** | You can't gamble against yourself |
| **Correct channel** | Must be in a gambling channel |

## Reading the Game Interface

During a gamble, the bot displays:

- **Game type** and **format** at the top
- **Current score** (in BO3/BO5 matches)
- **Wager amount** visible throughout
- **Interactive buttons** for your moves
- **Roll results** and round outcomes in real time

## After the Match

When a match ends:
- **Winner** receives both stakes (2× the wager) added to their balance
- **Loser** has already had their wager deducted (it was locked at the start)
- Both players' gambling profiles are updated
- The result is logged to the gambling log channel

<Tip>
  Not sure which game to try first? **High/Low BO1** is the simplest and fastest. Pick HIGH or LOW, both roll, and the better roll wins. Quick and straightforward.
</Tip>
