Every gamble you play is tracked. Your personal gambling profile records your wins, losses, earnings, streaks, and more. The leaderboard lets you see how you stack up against everyone else.

## Gambling Profile

**Command:** `.gamblingprofile` (or `.gp`)

Your gambling profile is **private** — you can only view your own stats.

### What's Shown

| Section | Fields |
|---|---|
| **Overall Record** | Wins, Losses, Win Rate, W/L Ratio |
| **Earnings** | Net Profit, Total Volume Wagered, Average Bet Size |
| **Roll Stats** | Average Roll, Highest Roll, Lowest Roll |
| **Highlights** | Biggest Win, Worst Loss |
| **Streaks** | Current Win/Loss Streak, Best Win Streak, Worst Loss Streak |
| **Format Record** | BO1 W/L, BO3 W/L |
| **Activity** | Total Games, Unique Opponents, Current Streak |

### Understanding Your Stats

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Win Rate">
    Your win rate is calculated as `Wins / (Wins + Losses) × 100`. A 50% win rate means you're breaking even in terms of games won.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Net Profit">
    This is the total amount of Pokécoins you've won minus the total you've lost across all gambles. Positive means you're up overall; negative means you're down.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Volume">
    The total amount of Pokécoins you've wagered across all games — wins and losses combined. This shows how much action you've had.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Average Bet">
    Your total volume divided by the number of games played. This shows your typical wager size.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rank Emoji">
    Based on your total number of games played, you'll see a rank emoji on your profile:
    - 🥉 Bronze — getting started
    - 🥈 Silver — regular gambler
    - 🥇 Gold — experienced
    - 💎 Diamond — veteran
    - 👑 Crown — top-tier
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gambling Leaderboard

**Command:** `.gamblingleaderboard` (or `.glb`)

The leaderboard ranks all players across multiple categories.

### Categories

Use the dropdown menu to switch between categories:

| Category | What It Ranks |
|---|---|
| **Most Wins** | Total number of gambles won |
| **Most PC Won** | Total Pokécoins earned from gambling |
| **Highest Win Rate** | Win percentage (minimum 5 games to qualify) |
| **Most Games Played** | Total number of gambles played |
| **Net Profit** | Total earnings minus total losses |
| **Highest Volume** | Total Pokécoins wagered across all games |
| **Best Win Streaks** | Longest consecutive win streak |
| **Most Losses** | Total number of gambles lost |
| **Most PC Lost** | Total Pokécoins lost to gambling |

Each leaderboard page shows 10 players with navigation buttons to browse through the full rankings. Your own rank is highlighted when applicable.

<Note>
  The **Highest Win Rate** category requires a minimum of **5 games** played to qualify. This prevents someone with 1 win and 0 losses from topping the board.
</Note>

## Server Gambling Stats

**Command:** `.gamblestats` (or `.gstats`)

This shows aggregate statistics for the entire server:

| Stat | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Gambles** | Number of gambles completed server-wide |
| **Total Forfeits** | How many gambles ended in a forfeit |
| **PC Wagered** | Total Pokécoins put on the line across all gambles |
| **PC Transferred** | Total Pokécoins actually transferred (from losers to winners) |
| **Average Wager** | Average bet size across all gambles |
| **Game Breakdown** | Percentage played of each game type |
| **Format Breakdown** | Percentage played of each format |
| **Top Players** | Most wins, most losses, most PC won, most PC lost, longest streak |
| **Largest Gamble** | The single biggest wager in server history |
