The Helixia gambling system lets you challenge other players to one-on-one Pokécoin duels. Choose a game, pick your wager, and compete head-to-head. The winner takes both players' stakes.

## At a Glance

- **4 game types** to choose from
- **3 match formats** (Best of 1, Best of 3, Best of 5)
- **Minimum wager** of 50,000 PC
- **Secure escrow** — your coins are locked during the match so neither player can move them
- **Detailed stats tracking** — every game is recorded on your gambling profile

## Key Concepts

### Escrow System

When a gamble is accepted, both players' wagers are **locked** (escrowed). This means:

- You can't transfer or spend your wagered coins during the match
- You can't claim them through the claim panel
- They're completely secured until the match ends
- Winner gets both stakes (2× the wager)
- In case of a tie (rare, only in Buckshot), both stakes are returned

This prevents any manipulation during active matches.

### Forfeits

If a player takes too long on their turn (over 5 minutes), they automatically forfeit the match. The opponent wins and receives both stakes.

### One Session at a Time

You can only be in **one** gambling session at a time. If you have an active gamble, you can't start or accept another until it's resolved.
