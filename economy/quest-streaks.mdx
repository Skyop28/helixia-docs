The quest streak system rewards consistency. Complete all your daily quests every day and earn an escalating bonus on top of your regular quest rewards.

## How Streaks Work

Every time you complete **all three daily quests** in a single day, your streak advances by one day. The next day, if you complete all quests again, you move to the next streak day. If you miss a day, your streak resets to Day 1.

## Streak Rewards

| Streak Day | Bonus Reward |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | 10,000 PC |
| Day 2 | 20,000 PC |
| Day 3 | 30,000 PC |
| Day 4 | 40,000 PC |
| Day 5 | 50,000 PC |
| Day 6 | 60,000 PC |
| Day 7 | 70,000 PC |

The streak bonus is awarded each day when you complete all quests, in addition to the individual quest rewards and the bonus quest reward.

Over a full 7-day cycle, you earn a total of **280,000 PC** just from streak bonuses.

## Automatic Reset

Your streak resets under two conditions:

1. **You fail to complete all quests for a day.** If even one quest is incomplete when the day ends at midnight IST, your streak resets to Day 1.
2. **You complete Day 7.** After the seventh day, the streak automatically resets and you start over at Day 1 with a 10,000 PC bonus.

There is no way to "bank" or extend streaks beyond 7 days. The cycle always repeats: Day 1 through Day 7, then back to Day 1.

## Streak Reminders

If you're on an active streak and haven't completed all your quests yet, the bot will send you a **DM reminder at 8:00 PM IST** — four hours before the day ends.

The reminder tells you:
- What streak day you're on and what's at stake
- Which quests you still haven't completed
- That you have 4 hours left

<Note>
  You will only receive the reminder if you have an active streak (you completed all quests yesterday) and have at least one quest remaining today. If you've already finished everything, no reminder is sent.
</Note>

## Viewing Your Streak

Your current streak is displayed at the top of the `.quests` command output. It shows your current streak day and the bonus reward for that day.

<Tip>
  Streaks are one of the easiest ways to boost your daily earnings. Even on Day 1, the 10,000 PC bonus adds up over time — and by Day 7, you're earning an extra 70,000 PC on top of everything else.
</Tip>
