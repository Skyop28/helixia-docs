You can send Pokécoins directly to any other member in the server using the `.sendbal` command.

## How to Transfer

```
.sendbal @user amount
```

**Examples:**
```
.sendbal @Awnish 100000
.sendbal @Awnish 100k
.sendbal @Awnish 1.5m
```

The bot supports shorthand amounts:
- `k` = thousands (e.g., `50k` = 50,000)
- `m` = millions (e.g., `1m` = 1,000,000)

## Rules and Limits

| Rule | Details |
|---|---|
| **Minimum transfer** | 50,000 PC |
| **Cooldown** | 30 seconds between transfers |
| **Balance check** | You must have enough Pokécoins in your balance |
| **Self-transfer** | You cannot send Pokécoins to yourself |
| **Bot transfer** | You cannot send Pokécoins to bots |

<Warning>
  If you are in an **active gambling session**, your wagered amount is locked and cannot be transferred. This prevents any manipulation during ongoing gambles. The locked coins are returned to your balance once the gamble concludes.
</Warning>

## After Transferring

After a successful transfer:
- Your balance is reduced by the sent amount
- The recipient's balance increases by the same amount
- The transfer is logged and visible in your transfer history

You can view your transfer history by clicking the **Transfers** button on your `.info` profile.
