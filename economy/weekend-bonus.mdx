description: "Earn extra Pokécoins on the bonus quest every Saturday and Sunday."

Every weekend, the bonus quest reward gets a boost. Instead of the usual 150,000 PC for completing all three daily quests, you earn **200,000 PC** — an extra 50,000 PC just because it's the weekend.

## When Is It Active?

The weekend bonus is active on **Saturday and Sunday**, based on IST (Indian Standard Time). The bonus applies from midnight IST Saturday through 11:59 PM IST Sunday.

## How It Appears

In your `.quests` display, the bonus quest reward is shown as:

```
150,000 (+50,000) Weekend Bonus
```

This makes it clear that you're earning the standard 150,000 PC plus an additional 50,000 PC weekend boost.

## No Extra Action Required

The weekend bonus applies automatically. There's nothing special you need to do — just complete all three daily quests on Saturday or Sunday and you'll receive the boosted amount.

## Weekly Earnings Impact

Here's how the weekend bonus affects your total weekly quest earnings (not counting streak bonuses):

| Day | Bonus Quest Reward |
|---|---|
| Monday | 150,000 PC |
| Tuesday | 150,000 PC |
| Wednesday | 150,000 PC |
| Thursday | 150,000 PC |
| Friday | 150,000 PC |
| Saturday | **200,000 PC** |
| Sunday | **200,000 PC** |
| **Weekly Total** | **1,150,000 PC** |

That's an extra **100,000 PC per week** compared to having no weekend bonus.
