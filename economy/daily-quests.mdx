Daily quests are repeating tasks that reset every day at **midnight IST (Indian Standard Time)**. Completing them is the most reliable way to earn Pokécoins consistently.

## Viewing Your Quests

Run `.quests` (or `.dq`) to see your current daily quest progress. The display shows:

- A progress bar for each quest
- How close you are to completing each one
- The reward for each quest
- Your current quest streak
- Whether the bonus quest is available

## Active Quests

There are **three** daily quests plus a bonus quest for completing all three.

### Quest 1: Send 100 Messages

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Target** | 100 messages |
| **Reward** | 100,000 PC |
| **Where** | Whitelisted channel only |

Only messages sent in the server's designated tracked channel count toward this quest. Messages in other channels, including gambling channels or event channels, do not count.

<Tip>
  This is the same channel where your messages count toward mystery box rewards. Every 100 messages earns you both the quest reward and a mystery box.
</Tip>

### Quest 2: Participate in 1 Daily Event

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Target** | 1 event participation |
| **Reward** | 30,000 PC |
| **Events** | Roll Event, Tea Event, or Bingo Event |

You only need to participate in one event per day. You don't need to win — just participating is enough. The three types of events that count are:

- **Roll Event** — use the `.roll` command during a Roll Event
- **Tea Event** — react to the Mudae tea event start message within 15 seconds
- **Bingo Event** — be mentioned as a participant in a Sugari Beta bingo game

<Card title="Events Guide" icon="calendar-star" href="/events/overview">

</Card>

### Quest 3: Place 5 Auction Bids

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Target** | 5 auction bids |
| **Reward** | 25,000 PC |
| **Where** | Auction channel |

Place bids on items in the auction channel. Each bid counts as one toward the quest, regardless of the bid amount.

### Bonus Quest: Complete All Three

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Complete all 3 quests above |
| **Reward (Weekday)** | 150,000 PC |
| **Reward (Weekend)** | 200,000 PC |

Once all three individual quests are completed, the bonus quest automatically completes and you receive the bonus reward. On **weekends (Saturday and Sunday IST)**, the bonus is boosted to 200,000 PC — displayed in your quest view as `150,000 (+50,000) Weekend Bonus`.

## When Quests Reset

All quests reset at **12:00 AM IST (midnight)** every day. Any progress that isn't completed is lost — partial quest progress does not carry over.

<Warning>
  Make sure to complete and claim your quest rewards before midnight IST. Unclaimed progress resets with the new day.
</Warning>
